Amur Leopard - Critically Endangered

Amur leopards live in Russia and northeastern China. Fewer than 150 remain in the wild, making them extremely rare. Habitat loss and poaching for fur are the main threats. They are solitary and very good at hunting in cold forests. Conservation involves strict protected areas and anti-poaching measures

Javan Rhino - Critically Endangered

Javan rhinos are found only in one national park in Indonesia. They are shy and live in dense tropical forests. Fewer than 80 remain in the wild, making them critically endangered. Poaching and habitat loss are major threats. Efforts focus on strict protection and habitat management.
