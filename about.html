About | Faunet

WHY WE EXIST

Across the planet, animals are disappearing forever due to:

  • Habitat destruction

  • Poaching

  • Pollution

  • Climate change

  • Genetic isolation

WHAT WE DO

At Faunet, we focus on:

  • Simplified species info

  • Real facts instead of fear

  • Interactive learning

  • Quizzes to test knowledge

  • Showcasing critically endangered species first

OUR GOAL



“We want every visitor to leave with more awareness than they arrived with.”



Faunet isn’t just a website — It’s a movement for empathy + education + action.

HOW YOU CAN HELP

  • Learn & share facts

  • Support conservation groups

  • Report illegal wildlife trade

  • Reduce waste & use sustainable products

  • Donate if you can

  • Make wildlife part of everyday conversations
